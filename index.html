About the Project

Solving urban drainage pollution through technology

The Problem

Improper disposal of plastic, metal, paper, and other recyclables into public drainage systems causes flooding, water stagnation, mosquito breeding, and disease spread. Manual monitoring is slow and inefficient.

Our Solution

A digital platform that enables citizens to sell, donate, and recycle waste instead of dumping it. AI classifies waste from photos, ML predicts high-risk areas, and the reward system incentivizes eco-friendly behavior.
