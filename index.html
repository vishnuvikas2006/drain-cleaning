About the Project

Solving urban drainage pollution through technology

The Problem

Improper disposal of recyclables into public drainage causes flooding, water stagnation, mosquito breeding, and disease. Manual monitoring is slow and inefficient.

Our Solution

A digital platform where citizens sell, donate, and recycle waste instead of dumping it. AI classifies waste from photos, checks fair pricing, and rewards incentivise eco-friendly behaviour.
